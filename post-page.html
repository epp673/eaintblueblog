A Gem in My Playlist

Josee!

The song "Josee!" by Damons Year tells a heartfelt story about love, companionship, and cherishing every moment spent together. The lyrics describe how the singer's eyes widen when they look at the Moon. At the same time, they notice their reflection in the eyes of their loved one. This suggests a deep connection and a shared perspective on love.

Depicts a scene where beautiful shadows and blooming flowers surround the couple. They kiss passionately, cherishing each moment as if it were their last. The line "let's not grow old" emphasizes the desire to preserve their love and youthfulness. And the lyrics express a willingness to give everything they have in the name of love. The singer wants to provide

their loved one with everything they need, ensuring that there will be no regrets or sadness in their relationship. They are ready to give all their love and the remaining time they have. The lyrics also express a desire to hold their loved one again and brighten their world.The singer promises to cover everything that troubles them and be there to comfort them.

The phrase "Goodnight, my crying love" implies that the singer wants to reassure their loved one, expressing the depth of their love and devotion. Overall, "Josee!" conveys a message of unwavering love, cherishing precious moments, and committing to a lifelong partnership.

One cherished film in my collection

About Time

The movie's plot is emotionally intense, focusing on the importance of cherishing every moment of our lives with our beloved ones. The story is about Tim, a 21-year-old man who is unlucky in love and discovers that the men in his family have always had the ability to travel back in time.

Using his ability to travel back in time, he tries to make the best out of every day. However, unfortunate time-travel problems can happen. Going back in time will not be the same every time. The plot continues as he meets Mary, played by Rachel McAdams, who later becomes his wife. Tim uses his power to fix his mistakes,

ensuring a happy life with Mary and his loved ones. Yet, his unusual gift cannot save him from the sorrow and the unavoidable fact of death. The most emotional scene for me is the last scene, where he turns back time to live his life as a kid with his dad again. This scene was incredibly touching and it made me cry.

The movie's storytelling is outstanding, and the director has done a great job of keeping the audience engaged from beginning to end. The way the story runs and continues is so unique, and every scene where Tim goes back in time to fix incidents is reasonable. I think the movie shows how we can all learn to enjoy every second with our loved ones. It is so touching.

The movie's story is simple and well-organized. The casting is excellent, bringing the story to life. If you are looking for a heartwarming movie that will leave you feeling happy and fulfilled, About Time is for you.

적과의 동침

읽을면서 미소가 사라지지 않고 계속 웃 게 되는 귀염뽀짝한 글이다. 내용은 영어 능력 꽝은 고3 현정이를 위해 영어 과외 쌤을 어머니가 불러 주셨는데 문제는 그 과외 쌤 지연이 이제 막 고1이라는 거다. 현정이가 알아 채지 못하고 있다가 학교에서 지연과 마주치니 그제야 알았다 어찌저찌 잘 넘어가고 둘이 썸아닌 썸 타고 있는 와중 지연이한테 남친이 생겼다.

그 일이후 둘의 사이가 멀어졌더니 어느새 현정이 수능이라 더욱더 멀어 진다

졸업식날 남친과 이미 헤어진 지연이는 꽃다발을 들고 현정이를 찾아가는데 이미 서울로 올라갔단다. 그러나 둘은 1년뒤 현정이 대2 지연이 고3때 동거하면서 다시 만나게 된다. 대충 내용은 그런데 나중에 한국 시기가 좋을때 로코 장르 백합 드라마화로 나와도 괜찮을 것 같은 글이다. 꼭 그렇게 되길 바란다.
